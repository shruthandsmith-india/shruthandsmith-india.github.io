Welcome to Edu-Paedia

"Education is a natural process carried out by the child and is not acquired by listening to words but by experiences in the environment."

Vision : Serve children, parents and local communities with a purposeful, balanced approach to nurturing Active Minds, Healthy Bodies and Happy Hearts.

Our Mission : to provide a caring and nurturing environment where your child can learn and develop through chronological and developmental appropriate activities. Edu-Paedia programs offer peace of mind to each parent by providing the highest standards of childcare and education.

We believe that your child's early years they will learn the basic social, intellectual, emotional, and physical skills. These skills are essential for child's growth. Edu-Paedia strives to provide your child with these lifelong skills.

We also believe children below the age of eight learn best when they have:

  • Objects they can manipulate

  • Chances to safely explore the world around them

  • Opportunities to experiment and learn from trial and error.

Edu-Paedia is happy to engage with Apple certified professionals from Creative Infotech. These professionals will collaborate with Edu-Paedia team to include appropriate apple apps in the playschool learning activities.

Why Edu-Paedia

Here at 'Edu-paedia' we are following amalgamation of several methodologies, seeing the best interest of child.

1

Our Methodologies

Howard gardner's theory of multiple intelligence. As per Howard gardners multiple intelligence theory we at Edu-Paedia constantly work on below mentioned all the 9 intelligence:-

Linguistic Intelligence - Dramatic Play, Elocution and stage shows.

Mathematical and Logical Intelligence - Science experiments, Science light box , Puzzles, Shape sorter, cooking, gardening.

Visual and Spatial Intelligence - Cutting, Lacing and Scissoring

Bodily - Kinesthetic Intelligence - Jump and round play, music and movement, sand and water play, obstacle race.

Musical Intelligence - Interactive charts, sound cylinders, musical instruments & songs.

Interpersonal Intelligence - Lego & Blocks for constructive group play, clean up time.

Interpersonal Intelligence - Drama time, Morning Message, Reverence.

Spiritual - Caring for Environment, Sharing.

Naturalist - Gardening, uses of water, no circus/zoo - safari's instead

2

Montessori Method

We at Edu-Paedia truly believe to give our little genius enough independence to choose their daily activity, meanwhile we as a mentor will keep a close watch and correct them where ever needed. It improves the cognitive (the process of knowing, thinking, learning and judging) skill.

3

Theme Based Theory

Our entire session is divided in to 10 monthly themes. Our entire month curriculum will be based on these monthly themes. This is a beautiful approach to towards effective teaching. For example our June month theme would be 'All about me'

4

Reggio Emila Approach

We believe that every corner of the school must teach our children something new, as it is their 3rd teacher beside parents and mentor. Hence at Edu-Paedia we have introduced eight innovative corners for children's to explore and learn while playing. All our activity rooms are having some or the other information to project.

5

Play Way Method

Last but the most child friendly and widely used approach is play way method. As per this approach we teach all our young ones through play. The underwritten quote will explain everything.

'FOR CHILDREN THEIR PLAY IS THEIR WORK AND THEY STILL ENJOY IT.'
