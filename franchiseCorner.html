Franchisee Corner

Edu-Paedia for Excellence offers a complete business format franchise in the Pre School/kindergarten services. The unique franchise concept provides individuals with complete know how to build and operate a successful Pre School. Edu-Paedia offers its franchisees:-

Download Application Form

Franchise Concept & Support

  • A proven, well researched curriculum for Pre School with latest technology of Apple Ipad learning

  • Our Curriculum is an integrated learning, based on Montessori & Play way method

  • We offer a certified International Montessori teacher training course.

  • Content for Summer and Winter club programs

  • Training for the curriculum and the business process.

  • Continuous research and development

  • We offer a complete ecosystem on a web portal to manage Syllabus, Parent communication Diary, Timetable, accounts, Salary, Assessment, and Enquiries

Franchise Support

The Edu-Paedia franchise support includes:

Franchise orientation program (FOP)

A 2 week orientation program will be held for training the franchisee and their key personnel. The FOP aims at helping the franchisee understand the Pre School business and transferring the knowledge gathered by the Franchisor

Operations Management

Franchisee will be provided all the support required to run the operations of the school. This will include standard business operations, school administration, PR management etc.

Marketing support

The franchisor will provide requisite marketing support to propagate the various programs at Edu-Paedia through regional/national platforms. Sales & Marketing tools (brochures, leaflets, posters, audio visual aids) as and when developed will be sent as per policy

Supply of Equipment

Edu-Paedia will provide the required equipment as per the model of the Pre School.

On site Curriculum & counselors training

Curriculum training will be imparted to teachers on a quarterly basis in a given year. Additionally we provide specially developed training modules for counselors to enable them to convert prospective admissions.

Supply of Edu-Paedia ambience material

Franchisee will be provided the Edu-Paedia proprietary ambience material which would include standard furniture across all branches as well as the visual communication materials.

Curriculum

A web supported curriculum will be provided through CoolG

Ongoing support

The benefits of ongoing research and development will be transferred to the franchisee to keep abreast of the curriculum development

Franchise Profile

We are looking for committed partners who will help us carry forward our ambition of making learning a fun experience for kids. You must have

  • A college degree

  • A keen interest in education services

  • A love for working with young kids

  • Reasonable financial resources

  • A goal to be the pioneer,the educator to prepare children not just for school but for their journey in life.
